# Journal-Specific Figure Requirements

Quick reference for major journal figure specifications.

## Quick Reference Table

| Journal | Single Column | Double Column | Min DPI (photos) | Min DPI (line art) | Preferred Format |
|---------|---------------|---------------|------------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Nature | 89 mm (3.5") | 183 mm (7.2") | 300 | 1000 | EPS, PDF |
| Science | 55 mm (2.17") | 175 mm (6.89") | 300 | 1000 | EPS, PDF |
| Cell | 85 mm (3.35") | 178 mm (7.0") | 300 | 1000 | EPS, PDF |
| PLOS | 83 mm (3.27") | 173 mm (6.81") | 300 | 600 | EPS, TIFF |
| ACS | 82.5 mm (3.25") | 178 mm (7.0") | 300 | 600 | TIFF, EPS |

## Nature Portfolio

- **File formats**: PDF, EPS, AI (vector); TIFF, PNG (raster)
- **Color space**: RGB
- **Fonts**: Arial or Helvetica, minimum 5-7 pt
- **Panel labels**: a, b, c (lowercase, bold, top-left)
- **Scale bars**: Required for microscopy
- **Source data**: Required for all graphs

## Science (AAAS)

- **File formats**: EPS, PDF (vector); TIFF (raster)
- **Color space**: RGB
- **Fonts**: Helvetica or Arial, 6-8 pt minimum
- **Panel labels**: (A), (B), (C) in parentheses
- **Error bars**: Required; define in caption

## Cell Press

- **File formats**: PDF, EPS (vector); TIFF (raster)
- **Color space**: RGB
- **Fonts**: Arial or Helvetica only
- **Resolution**: 1000 DPI line art, 300 DPI photographs

## PLOS

- **File formats**: EPS, PDF (vector); TIFF, PNG (raster)
- **Color space**: RGB
- **Fonts**: Sans-serif preferred
- **Special**: Figures should be understandable without caption

## ACS

- **File formats**: TIFF, PDF, EPS
- **Color space**: RGB or CMYK
- **Fonts**: Embedded fonts required
- **Panel labels**: a, b, c (lowercase, bold)
