# Conclusion — Capstone, Not Summary

Load this module when drafting or reviewing a Conclusion.

Distinct from the Abstract — the Conclusion is the capstone, not a summary.

- **Restate the nugget** — not the method. What did we *learn*?
- **Synthesize, don't list**: Don't enumerate results — weave them into a coherent narrative about what changed in our understanding
- **Forward-looking**: What are the implications? What questions remain?
- **No new information**: Every claim must be supported by results presented earlier
