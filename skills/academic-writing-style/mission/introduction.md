# Introduction — Funnel Structure

Load this module when drafting or reviewing an Introduction section.

## Funnel Structure

```
Broad context (1–2 paragraphs)
    ↓ What field? Why does it matter?
Narrowing to specific problem (1–2 paragraphs)
    ↓ What is known? What are the key prior approaches?
Identify the gap (1 paragraph)
    ↓ What is missing / unsolved / contradictory?
State your contribution (1 paragraph)
    ↓ What did you do? What is the nugget?
Outline the paper (1 short paragraph, optional for short papers)
    ↓ "Section 2 describes... Section 3 presents..."
```

## Rules

- Every claim must cite a source (reviews for broad context, original research for specific findings)
- The gap must be precise — not "few studies" but "no prior study has measured X under Y conditions controlling for Z"
- The contribution paragraph must state the **nugget** — the single key insight
- Outline paragraph is optional in short papers (≤4 pages), mandatory in theses
