# Limitations — Strategic Placement

Load this module when reviewing or placing limitations statements.

Placement depends on document type:

- **Peer-reviewed papers**: Be strategic. Don't expose weaknesses prematurely.
  - Acknowledge briefly with a mitigation: "While X assumes Y, this can be mitigated by Z"
  - Or frame as future work rather than weakness

- **Thesis / internal documents**: Discuss limitations earlier, at the point the design decision is made, showing the reader awareness of assumptions and tradeoffs.

**Never**: defensive tone; limitations listed without mitigation; limitations in Methods before the reader has seen results.
