# Final Review Checklist

## Structure & Narrative
- [ ] Every section follows GPS (Goal → Problem → Solution)?
- [ ] All paragraphs chained with explicit transitions?
- [ ] Section intros match what subsections deliver?
- [ ] Every paragraph ends with a closer, not a trailing sentence?
- [ ] Can the nugget be stated in one sentence?

## Prose & Style
- [ ] No filler phrases (it is important to note, due to the fact that, etc.)
- [ ] No "not X, but Y" negation-contrast structures
- [ ] No AI-tell words (delve, leverage, landscape, tapestry, multifaceted)
- [ ] Calibrated confidence: assertive for facts, hedged for mechanisms
- [ ] One idea per sentence
- [ ] Active voice for analysis; passive only in Methods and specific contexts

## Figures & Tables
- [ ] Every float cross-referenced and discussed in body text
- [ ] Captions self-sufficient without body text
- [ ] Figure-text-caption consistency verified

## Citations & Bibliography
- [ ] All named methods/datasets cited at first use per section
- [ ] Every scientific claim traced to a source
- [ ] Bibliography entries complete (authors, title, year, journal, volume, pages, DOI)
- [ ] Consistent formatting across all entries
- [ ] arXiv preprints updated to published versions where available
