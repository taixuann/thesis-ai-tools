# 📝 /write-intro: Introduction & Literature Review

This workflow guides the preparation of a scientific or technical paper's Introduction. It uses a **Broad-to-Narrow funnel** structure to establish why the research matters, what current solutions lack, how the proposed work addresses these shortcomings, and the explicit objectives of the study.

---

## 📋 Academic Layout Structure

1. **Global Context (The "Why"):**
   - Introduce the high-level technical, societal, or scientific challenge.
   - Establish the scale of the problem using concrete metrics (e.g., energy consumption, physical limits, efficiency bottlenecks).
2. **State-of-the-Art (SotA) Limitations:**
   - Review current leading methods, materials, or devices.
   - Detail their specific shortcomings, defects, high operational costs, or stability issues.
3. **The Proposed Solution/Approach:**
   - Introduce the proposed candidate material, system, or theoretical mechanism.
   - Explain its fundamental physical, chemical, or engineering properties.
   - Explain why this properties set is uniquely suited to overcome the SotA limitations.
4. **Objectives & Contributions:**
   - List the specific, measurable goals of this study (e.g., synthesis kinetics, performance optimization, model validation).
   - Summarize the novel scientific contributions of the work.

## 🔬 Data-Gathering Questionnaire

Copy the questionnaire below, fill it in with raw details, and paste it into the chat to begin drafting:

```markdown
### Introduction Data Gathering
- **Global Challenge & Motivation:** [e.g., energy consumption of traditional computational architectures]
- **Key Motivating Metrics:** [e.g., brain's 20W power vs. megawatt silicon clusters]
- **State-of-the-Art (SotA) Systems:** [e.g., conventional inorganic memristors]
- **SotA Shortcomings:** [e.g., high forming voltages, rigid lattices, poor biocompatibility]
- **Proposed Solution/System:** [e.g., organic mixed conductor polydopamine (PDA)]
- **Key Supporting Mechanisms:** [e.g., protonic-electronic mixed conduction via hydrogen bonding]
- **Specific Objectives (List 2-4):**
  1. [e.g., Characterize pH-modulated electropolymerization kinetics]
  2. [e.g., Achieve stable threshold switching below 0.5V]
  3. [e.g., Verify quinone/catechol ratios using in-situ Raman/SERS]
```

---

## ✍️ Execution Steps

1. **Questionnaire Review:** The AI checks the completed questionnaire for physical parameters, clarity, and quantitative benchmarks.
2. **Draft Outline:** The AI generates a section-by-section outline mapping the gathered data to the layout.
3. **Collaborative Drafting:** Polish sections one-by-one under the C.A.R.E. drafting protocol, keeping the human's authentic scientific voice and postponing citations to Phase 3.
