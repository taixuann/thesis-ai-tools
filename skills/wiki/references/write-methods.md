# 🔬 /write-methods: Materials & Methods Playbook

This workflow guides the creation of a chronological, recipe-like Methodology section. It ensures the writing uses the **third-person passive voice** and contains enough physical, chemical, and instrumentation detail to make the study fully reproducible.

---

## 📋 Academic Layout Structure

1. **Materials, Reagents & Preparation:**
   - Source chemical grades, purities, solvent specifications, and buffer preparation.
   - Substrate cleaning protocols (e.g., sonication, chemical treatments, degassing/purging).
2. **Synthesis, Fabrication, or Experimental Setup:**
   - Chronological fabrication steps.
   - Precise parameters: temperature profiles, pressures, currents, sweep ranges, cycle counts, scan rates, and flow rates.
3. **Post-Treatment or State Conditioning:**
   - Thermal treatment (annealing temperature, time, and atmosphere/vacuum levels).
   - Electrical, chemical, or mechanical conditioning/doping states.
4. **Physical, Chemical & Electrical Characterization:**
   - Measurement techniques and protocols (microscopy, spectroscopy, transport curves).
   - Instrument manufacturers, models, excitation sources, powers, exposure times, and scan rate bounds.

---

## 🔬 Data-Gathering Questionnaire

Copy the questionnaire below, fill it in with raw details, and paste it into the chat to begin drafting:

```markdown
### Methodology Data Gathering
- **Starting Materials & Reagents:** [e.g., Dopamine hydrochloride 99% Sigma, ITO substrates]
- **Substrate Pre-treatment:** [e.g., sonication in acetone/IPA for 15 mins, nitrogen dry]
- **Synthesis/Fabrication Steps:** [e.g., electrochemical electropolymerization in 3-electrode cell]
- **Exact Fabrication Parameters:** [e.g., pH 6.0 PBS, scan rate 20 mV/s, -0.5 to +0.5V sweep, 10 cycles]
- **Post-Treatments / Annealing:** [e.g., vacuum annealing at 80°C for 30 minutes at 10^-3 Torr]
- **Characterization Techniques:** [e.g., Raman spectroscopy, I-V curves using Keithely 2400]
- **Instrument Specs & Settings:** [e.g., 785 nm laser, 5 mW power, 20s exposure; I-V sweep rate 50 mV/s]
```

---

## ✍️ Execution Steps

1. **Voice Audit:** The AI verifies the writing uses third-person passive voice (e.g., "The substrate was cleaned..." instead of "We cleaned the substrate...").
2. **Replication Audit:** The AI reviews the methodology to ensure no critical parameters (temperatures, flow rates, concentrations) are missing.
3. **Collaborative Drafting:** Polish each subsection to achieve absolute clarity and conciseness.
